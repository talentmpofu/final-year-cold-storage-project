Emergency Override Mode

You are about to disable automatic PID control. These controls override automatic regulation and should only be used for testing, maintenance, or emergency situations.

When you switch to manual mode:

  • The system will stop automatically regulating temperature, humidity, and air quality
  • You will have full manual control over cooling, humidifier, and scrubbing systems
  • Produce quality may be at risk if manual settings are incorrect
  • The system normally manages all components automatically based on sensor readings

Only proceed if you understand the risks and need manual control.
